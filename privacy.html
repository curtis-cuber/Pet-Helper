Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

What We Collect

When you post a lost pet alert, we store the information you fill in (pet name, description, contact info, photo link, and last seen location). If you create an account, we store your email address.

How We Use It

We use your information only to display your lost pet alert on the site and help neighbors contact you. We do not sell your information to anyone.

Public Information

Everything you put in a lost pet alert is public — anyone visiting the site can see it. Only share contact details you are comfortable making public.

Location

We use your approximate location (from your IP address) to pre-fill location fields and show nearby pets. This is never stored.

Deleting Your Data

If you want your posts or account removed, contact us and we will delete them.
