# Pet Helper – Project Summary

## Audience
Pet owners who have just lost their pet and need help fast.

## Problem
Paper flyers are unreliable — wind blows them away and most neighbors never see them.

## Solution
A website where pet owners can post a digital "missing pet" alert with a photo and description, so neighbors in the area can easily see it and keep an eye out.

---

## Key Insights (from planning questions)

- When a pet goes missing, the owner's first instinct is to search for help online.
- The hardest part of finding a lost pet is **spreading the word** — sharing what the pet looks like, where it was last seen, and alerting people to watch for it.
- The main user is the **pet owner**, not the person who finds a stray.
- Paper flyers fail because they blow away and have limited reach.
