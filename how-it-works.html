How Pet Helper Works

Three simple steps to help find a missing pet.

1

Create an Account

Sign up for a free account so you can post lost pet alerts and keep track of your posts.

2

Post a Lost Pet Alert

Fill in your pet's name, photo, description, color, animal type, and where they were last seen. Your alert goes live immediately.

3

Neighbors Keep an Eye Out

Anyone can browse all missing pet alerts. If a neighbor spots your pet, they click "I Found This Pet" and you get an email right away.

4

Leave Tips

Neighbors can also leave comments on any post — for example, "I saw a dog matching this description near the park on Monday."

5

Mark as Found

Once your pet is home safe, mark your post as found. The card turns green so the community knows the good news.
